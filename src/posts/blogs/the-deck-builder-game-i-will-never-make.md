I think it's fairly common that a lot of SWE these days got started on this career journey because they wanted to make a video game. I really tried my hardest but  It takes a lot of discipline to finish a game, so I respect a lot of solo and indie devs finishing it.

Deck Builders are my favorite kind of games, and combine that in a video game I am sold. And I tried not only making my own game like the OG Dream Quest, but when I saw and played Slay the Spire for the first time they literally perfected that formula perfectly. In my head I think "wow I can combine Dream Quest and StS", I really think there IS something there, but StS is just so much better... and I hate to say this, but when other devs make another StS like game, I give it a shot...and it's just... why would i play this when StS is just SO much better... I am sorry
